About

I'm Amy, a product designer at Buildertrend. My work focuses on the parts of the platform where builders send invoices, track job financials, and walk clients through selections — the interfaces that carry the weight of real financial decisions.

I care about the details: the clarity of a number, the confidence of a moment, the difference between a tool people tolerate and one they actually rely on.

Most of what I do comes out of talking to the builders who live inside these tools every day. GCs, remodelers, custom home builders, office managers — the kind of people who'll tell you in the first five minutes why the software is broken. Their feedback shapes almost every design decision I make.

Outside of work, you'll find me on a long walk, at the gym, or squeezing in a fishing trip whenever I can.
