About

I'm a product designer at Buildertrend, where I focus on simplifying the financial and operational workflows builders rely on to run their businesses.

My work spans research, interaction design, and systems thinking — designing for trust in interfaces that handle the weight of real decisions. I care about the details: the clarity of a number, the confidence of a moment, the difference between a tool people tolerate and one they actually rely on.

I spend a lot of time talking to the people who live inside our tools every day — GCs, remodelers, custom home builders — and turning what I learn into cleaner, clearer, more confident workflows.
